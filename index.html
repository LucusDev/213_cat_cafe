About Us

Welcome to Mori

Welcome to Mori, the cat cafe of your dreams! Our cozy cafe is a sanctuary for cat lovers and cats alike. Come cuddle with our playful cats while sipping on your favorite coffee or tea. Our mission is to provide a relaxing and stress-free environment for both cats and humans. Come visit us at Mori and experience the magic of coffee and cats!

Our cat pick-up service is designed to help busy cat owners by providing a safe and caring environment for their cat friends when they're unable to take care of them themselves. We carefully select friendly and playful cats from our cafe and transport them to our facility where they receive the attention and care they deserve. Our cat pick-up service gives cat owners peace of mind, knowing their pets are in good hands.
