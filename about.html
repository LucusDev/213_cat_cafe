Welcome to Mori.

Welcome to Mori Cat Cafe, where you can relax, unwind, and cuddle with some of the cutest and friendliest cats around. Our cozy cafe is the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and spend some quality time with our adorable feline residents.

At Mori Cat Cafe, we're more than just a coffee shop - we're a community of cat lovers who are passionate about providing a safe and loving environment for our furry friends. Our cats are all rescues and come from local shelters, and we take pride in providing them with the love, care, and attention they deserve.

At Mori Cat Cafe, we believe that cats can have a positive impact on our mental health, and we're committed to providing a space where both cats and humans can benefit from this special bond. So why not come and visit us today? We can't wait to welcome you to our cozy little corner of the city.

We offer a variety of drinks and snacks to enjoy while you spend time with our cats, including delicious coffee and tea options, as well as a selection of pastries and snacks. We also offer specialty items like our famous cat-themed latte art, which is almost too cute to drink!

In addition to our cafe offerings, we also provide pick up and booking services for busy cat owners who want to ensure that their pets are well taken care of when they can't be there. Our team of trained professionals will ensure that your cat is safe, happy, and well-fed, so you can enjoy peace of mind knowing that your furry friend is in good hands.
